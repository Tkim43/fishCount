The Pursuit 1/2 Day

Wed Nov 07 2018, 04:50PM by Sea Monkey Good afternoon folks. Just heading back to the dock after a very fun day of fishing in gorgeous weather. We checked out a new zone this morning and they were on the bite. Big chucks, reds, lings with a few groupers and other miscellaneous rockfish in the deep water. We then shot into the shallows and topped off our bags with some nice grade whitefish, sheephead, calicos and a really good showing on them barracudas with a few legals. Total count for our 25 anglers: 36 lingcod, 204 coppers, 12 vermillions, 34 miscellaneous rockfish, 140 whitefish, 4 sheephead, 3 calicos, and 7 barracuda.
